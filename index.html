About Me

Once upon a time, far from sense of man and the logic of reason, there lived a man who made things.

I have studied visual art academically for over 10 years, and am very comfortable with the create, critique, and collaboration process it requires. With over half of my formal education spent studying Fine Arts; I can confidently say I have a very solid foundation in the fundamentals of traditional art and understand how greatly it impacts full comprehension of a digital medium.
